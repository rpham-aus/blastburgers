    Are vegetarian burgers healthier?
    Vegetarian burgers are mostly plant-based and they tend to be a lot healthier for you except for store-bought veggie patties. Some veggie burgers can include a lot of artificial ingredients that make them less healthy than a regular burger. Which is why we include our full ingredients list, nutritional information and allergen information for all our products.
